PyTom: Align subtomograms

  • "Old" Expectation-Maximization based Real Space Alignment

    This alignment algorithm was the default until 2015 and originally published in Hrabe et al, JSB 2012.

  • "New" Real Space Alignment

    To incorporate Gold Standard FSC for resolution determination and filtering of iteratively improved averages we re-engineered the alignment algorithm in 2015. The usage from the command line is greatly simplified, but currently no GUI support is provided.
